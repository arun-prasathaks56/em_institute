Diploma In X-Ray Technician

ABOUT

X-ray technology courses are the most popular courses among students and of great importance in medical treatment. Students are taught about the types of X-Ray, imaging technology used, ways to read an x-ray, which x-ray to use in a particular case, etc. The course is delivered by our highly qualified and dynamic trainers who bring the best practices in learning to equip the students with industry-relevant skills and enhance their employability skills

Our institute replicate the real hospitals and are well equipped with modern facilities for hand-on training in X-ray and electrocardiogram procedure. The two year program consists of theory classes, practical training and paid on-job-training in reputed healthcare set ups.
