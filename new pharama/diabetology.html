Diploma in Optometry

ABOUT

Diploma in Optometry course includes eye and related structures, as well as vision, visual systems & vision information processing in humans. Optometrists are primary health care providers for the Diagnosis, management, and treatment of Eye Diseases, Refractive disorders. This course applicant is familiar with inspection and analyzing of numerous refractive conditions and oversees Optical guides, for example, Optical Glasses, Contact lenses, and other Remedial guides

Students can also specialize in the field of Contact lenses, Orthotics, Low Vision Aids, Sports Vision, Pediatric Optometry and Behavioral Optometry especially as it associates with vision and the eye. The course is a career offering in nature that opens many scopes for the applicants both in the private and government sectors
