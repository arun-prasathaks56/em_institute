Physician Assistant

ABOUT

Physician Assistants are formally trained to provide diagnostic, therapeutic and preventive health care services in all the medical specialties, as delegated by a physician. Working as members of a health care team, they collect medical histories, initial examination of the patients, order for laboratory tests and x-rays and assist the doctors for diagnosis and treatment

The Physician Assistants work in different areas such as outpatient wing, wards and critical care units, operation theatres and other super specialty hospital units. The scope of this course is tremendous in our country. Apart from working as Physician Assistant in hospitals, they can get placements in medical software institutions, medical tourism, and medical insurance and medical research
